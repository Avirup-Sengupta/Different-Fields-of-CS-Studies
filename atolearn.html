Skills You Need To Be An AI Engineer

AI is a very technical field. Math, electronics, instrumentation, programming all go hand in hand. Hence for someone who is new in the industry it will be wise to gain experience in some other fields of CS like Web Development before moving to AI or ML.

Degree in Math/Finance/Economics/CS/Electronics/Statistics in kind of must for this domain.

Other necessary skills are:

  • High School Mathematics with mastery in Calculus,Linear Algebra,Statistics,Probability and Combinatorics

  • Strong expertise in programming language link Python/R and another object oriented programming language like Java/C++/C#

  • Technology Tools and infrastructure.

  • Knowledge of Database.

  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning Algorithms.

  • Knowledge of relevant framework.

  • Critical Thinking, Analytical Sense, Business Knowledge is also important.
