What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a unambigious step by step instruction having finite number of steps to solve a problem.

It can be expressed in form of pseducode, flowchart or just simple step by step write-up.

Not only in competetive programming but algorithms are building blocks of computer science. They are derived from certain mathematical results and written in such a manner that it will solve a certain problem when implemented through Computer Programming. Algorithms are everywhere. Every programmer should have good understanding of algorithms to solve problems efficiently and write code.

We will see different algorithms specially inclined on Competetive Programming.
