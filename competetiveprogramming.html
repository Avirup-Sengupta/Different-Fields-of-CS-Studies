Competetive Programming



Brief Introduction

Competetive programming is a mind sport. Here a number of problems are provided to the contestants under strict time constrain. The contestants needs to solve those problems using different algorithms

The algorithms are well known to computer science and one just needs to trick and twik those to arrive at a solution.

Having strong math background is also an advantage apart from knowing a programming language. Problem solving knack is also essential as it will help one to continue doing the task in the long run. It is also quite fun to do. When you pass a solution there arrives a green trick which gives the dopamine rush. Let's see the trajectory we have to follow to be a successful competetive programmer.
